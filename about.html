About Our School

The purpose of our school is to help students realize their value, accomplish their goals, and improve their lives.



Our School History


Learning Skill School has been registered as an RTO since 2006 and is a not-for-profit organisation with significant experience in the delivery of quality training and assessment services. Learning Skill School specialise in the delivery of training and assessment for traditional trade areas, spanning from Certificate I through to Diploma level qualifications. With an expertise developed over more than 13 years of operation, Learning Skill School is a leader in providing industry driven training to learners, supported by ongoing engagement and liaison with industry partners and employers. Learning Skill School aim to always provide training that meets the evolving skills needs of Australian industry and employers. Australian Trade Training College has extensive experience in partnering with community based organisations providing training and assessment services for Queensland State Government funded programs. Learning Skill School employs a wide staff base with extensive experience in the VET sector, with particular niche knowledge of their respective trade areas. All Learning Skill School College staff are qualified in their respective industries, demonstrate a high level of expertise in the delivery of training and assessment and hold industry licencing and qualifications appropriate to their trade.
